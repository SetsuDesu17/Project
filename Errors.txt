--- Errors ---

java.io.IOException: Failed to download remote update

If you are connected to the same network, connected your phone on ur laptop with usb tethering on and this error still persist, its usually Microslop's fault (Fuck u windows security)
To fix it:
1. Go to Windows Security
2. Go to Firewall & Nework Protection (For reference, open README_Assets/Firewall&NetworkProtection folder)
3. Go to Advanced Settings
4. Go to Inbound Rules
5. Press New Rule...
6. Select Port
7. Select TCP and Specific local ports, insert your Expo Go's port
8. Press Next till you reach the Name
9. For convenience's sake, name it ExpoGo
